Iphone X

wikimedia

This is the Iphone x. it was the tenth iphone and a very big milestone. portrait mode with the front camera was a large additon.

The iphone X took away the home button and improved upon the camera by adding an extra front facing camera. The Iphone X let you take portrait mode pictures with the front facing camera for the first time.
